The Job

As a text chat operator you will be answering casual messages on a fantasy-based text network. You have to be able to talk to customers about all kinds of topics. You will be assigned different roles, depending on what your customer wants.

Some of our clients just want to talk to someone about everyday life. Small talk about things like work, culture, weather, relationships. Your identity will not be known by customers because you will be assumed to be the person the customer is texting to.

Depending on how much time and effort you put in, an average operator earns around 300400 USD a week. This can also be a lot more if you are willing to go the extra mile. We supply bonuses when you write a lot of messages and extra extensions on weekends and red days.

  • Anonymous chat only, no calling or video
  • Work anytime, anywhere and as much as you want!
  • No qualifications necessary
  • Support available 24/7

    What you need to know before applying:

    You need to be fluent in English

    As you will be writing text to native English speakers you must know how to write in English fluently. We will do a basic test to check your level of English as part of the application process.

    You must be at least 18 years old

    We do not accept minors to work on the chat platform. As part of the application process you will be asked to prove your age.

    You need a PC or laptop

    In order to be an effective chat operator you need to have access to a laptop to work from. The device can be very basic and there is no need for it to have a camera.


    You do not need prior experience

    We will teach you everything you need to know, and our support team is available 24/7 whilst you work. We will NOT ask you for your resume in the application process.

    You need reliable internet connection

    All work is done online connected via the internet. The more stable your internet connection is, the better your experience will be working as a chat operator.

    You will work as a Freelancer

    As a chat agent you are self-employed. We will assist you in setting up how to receive payments for your work. All payments are done weekly through a reputable online payment provider.

    You need an updated personal ID card

    An important part of the application process is the ID verification check.

    Why do we ask for your personal ID?

    When you apply for a job at our company, we need to check that you are who you say you are.

    We do this for three main reasons:
    • To check if you are old enough to work for us (18+)
    • To prevent fraud
    • To confirm your identity, so that we can process payments to the right person

    We do this by using an external service to check the ID. When everything is checked and verified correctly, we will allow you to proceed with your application. To ensure this process runs smoothly, please sign up using your name as it appears on your ID.
